Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

Better Metal Roofs collects information to provide better services to all our users. We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you request a quote or contact our office in Ware, MA. This may include your name, contact details, and project specifications for metal roof installation or repair.

2. Use of Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our award-winning roofing services.
  • Process your "Get a Quote" requests and provide project updates.
  • Communicate with you regarding our services, including heated roof systems and ice-dam retrofits.

3. Data Sharing & Partnerships

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with specialized partners, such as Heatedroofsystems.com, to facilitate the design and installation of specific roofing solutions as requested by the client.

4. Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access. As an industry leader with over 20 years of experience, we prioritize the integrity of our client relationships.
